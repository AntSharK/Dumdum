OBJECTIVE

You own a ball. Every round, your ball goes into an arena with other balls.
The objective is to be the last player in the game.
Every round, every player except the top person loses points.
Ranking is determined by your ball's total HP left. Balls eliminated early lose more points.
When you get to 0 points, you are out of the game.

FIGHTING

When two balls collide, each ball does damage to the other ball.
Damage dealt gives you credits.
Damage dealt can be reduced by armor and other upgrades.

USER INTERFACE

The cost of each upgrade is displayed on the top-right of the card.
The number of credits you have left will be displayed in the top-right.
Click on an upgrade card to buy it. Buying a card will refresh the shop.
Pressing the refresh icon will also refresh the shop. Refreshing costs 1 credit.
Clicking on your ball will alternate between showing your stats and showing notable upgrades.

BUYING UPGRADES

You can buy upgrades that you can't afford. The debt will be collected on the next round.
Unspent credits at the end of every round will be thrown away.
At the end of every round, you will get more credits. The amount you are given increases per round.
You start with a "Payday" upgrade which gives you credits when you do damage.
As long as you have credits, you can continue buying upgrades after the round starts. However, they won't apply until the next round.
If, on the previous round, you spent more credits than you had, they will be deducted from you on the next round.

UPGRADING THE SHOP

Upgrading the shop gives you better upgrades.
This adds the better upgrades into the pool - old upgrades will stil show up.
Upgrading the shop also allows you to see more upgrades per refresh.
